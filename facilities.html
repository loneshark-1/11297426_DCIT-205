Facilities

Welcome To Our Facilities

The computer science facilities in the school contribute significantly to the holistic development of students, providing them with the tools and environments they need to excel academically, socially, and physically.

These are some facilities in the computer science department we can talk about;

Computer Science Laboratory

This laboratory provides students with access to computers and the internet, fostering digital literacy and competency.

It also helps students learn essential skills such as typing, basic computer operations, and software usage, which are increasingly important in the modern world.

Teaching assistant are mostly found in computer labs which are ideal spaces for teaching programming and coding skills.

The Library

The Computer science library often contains materials specifically tailored to the subjects and courses offered by the department

Specialized journals, reference books, and research publications can enhance the depth of knowledge available to students and researchers.

The Computer science library serves as a hub for collaboration and intellectual exchange within the department.

Sports Field

All departmental sports activities are held at the sports field

These sports activities are associated with improved health, increased energy levels, and better stress management.

Team sports promote collaboration, communication, and a sense of camaraderie among participants, fostering a positive and supportive departmental culture.

These sports activities provide a relaxed and informal setting for faculty and students to interact outside of the academic environment.
